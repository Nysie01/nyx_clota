Welcome to CLOTA SHOP, where everyday style meets effortless comfort. Our business began in Olongapo, Central Luzon, Philippines. We started as a passion project for people who were tired of fast fashion that falls apart after one wash. In 2024, we launched with a simple mission: to create high-quality, affordable clothing that makes you feel confident and carefree, no matter the occasion.At Clota Shop, every piece is thoughtfully designed with real life in mind. Our company uses sustainable materials which provide comfort during tropical weather because we use breathable cottons and soft linens and durable blends which withstand everyday use. Our collection features Filipino fashion through casual tees and flowy dresses and versatile jackets and statement accessories. We offer complete inclusivity by providing sizes that accommodate all body types and fashion options that match every individual style.


Quality first: Handpicked materials and ethical production—no compromises.
Local love: Proudly supporting Filipino artisans and small-batch makers.
Affordable joy: Stylish looks without the hefty price tag.
